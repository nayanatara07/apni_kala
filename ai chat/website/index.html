Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I become an Apni Kala artisan?

We are always looking for talented artisans to join our platform. Please visit our website for information on how to apply.

2. What are the benefits of selling on Apni Kala?

Apni Kala offers a platform to showcase your work to a global audience, gain fair compensation, and connect with customers who appreciate your craftsmanship. We also provide training and support to help you grow your business.

3. How will Apni Kala help me market my products?

Apni Kala utilizes various marketing channels to promote our platform and your products. This includes online marketing, social media campaigns, and collaborations with influencers.

4. What is Apni Kala's social impact?

Apni Kala empowers underrepresented artisans, preserves cultural heritage, promotes economic growth in communities, and encourages sustainable practices.

5. What kind of training and support does Apni Kala offer?

We provide training programs on various aspects of running a successful online business, including product photography, marketing, and social media management. We also offer mentorship opportunities to connect you with experienced professionals.
